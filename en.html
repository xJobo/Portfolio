About

Finding what's broken where everything seems to work.

In 2017, WannaCry paralyzed hospitals and businesses worldwide. I was eleven, and one question struck me: how can such critical systems be so vulnerable? That question never left me.

What drives me in cybersecurity is the rigor it demands. Finding a vulnerability isn't about luck — it's a methodology, a way of thinking. Pentest labs with Kali Linux and Metasploit, CTFs on TryHackMe, Cisco certifications alongside my studies.

Currently pursuing an engineering degree at EFREI, majoring in Networks & Security, I keep pushing forward: offensive security, networking, and hands-on practice.
